My Top Five Favourite Movies

My top five(5) favourite movies are:

  1. Titanic

    Of the estimated 2, 224 passengers and crew aboard, 1, 496 died, making the incident the deadliest sinking of a single ship.

  2. Coming To America

    Coming To America is a 1998 American romantic comedy film.
    Eddie Murphy plays Akeem Joffer, the crown prince of fictionalAfrican nation of Zamunda.

  3. Passion Of Christ

    This is a 2004 American Epic Biblical film, co-written by Mel Gibson, which teaches love, hope, faith, and forgiveness.
    This movie portrays the last days and crucifixion of Jesus Christ.

  4. A Walk To Remember

    This is a 2002 film, adapted from a novel with same name. It is based off a novel by an American author Nicholas Sparks.
    This is a story of two teenagers who fall in love with each other, despite the disparity of their personalities.

  5. Sound Of Music

    Maria, an aspiring nun, is sent as a governess to take care of seven motherless children.
    Soon, her jovial and loving nature tames their hearts and the children become fond of her.
